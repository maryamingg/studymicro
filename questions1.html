Microbiology Questions - Page 1

Question 1: What is microbiology?

Microbiology is the study of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa.

Question 2: What are the main types of microorganisms?

The main types of microorganisms include bacteria, viruses, fungi, and algae.

Question 3: What is the role of bacteria in the ecosystem?

Bacteria play crucial roles in nutrient cycling, such as decomposing organic matter and fixing nitrogen.

Question 4: What is a pathogen?

A pathogen is any microorganism that causes disease in a host organism.
